Introduction
Currently, I am an associate professor at the College of Computer Science and Technology, Qingdao University. I received my BS degree in computer science in 2010 and my MS and PhD degrees in computer applications technology from the Ocean University of China in 2012 and 2015, respectively. My current research interests involve variational methods, partial differential equations, and numerical optimization, and with applications to image/video processing and image/video quality assessment.
